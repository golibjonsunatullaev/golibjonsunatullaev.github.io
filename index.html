Hi, I'm Golibjon Sunatullaev

I'm an M.S. student in Data Science and a member of the HAI Lab at Kangwon National University, advised by Prof. Auk Kim. I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Kangwon National University. My research interests include Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), ubiquitous computing, and human-centered sensor-driven systems that support interruptibility-aware intelligent systems and proactive services in smart home environments.
